## Find your License
A common request is how to find your license information and registration key, for use with a new machine or a lost registration for an existing machine.  

License information is stored with your orders in your West Wind Store account:

[Log into your West Wind Store Account](https://store.west-wind.com/profile/)

Orders are listed at the bottom of your user profile. Click into into the specific order and then click **Resend Confirmations** to resend the license and download information confirmation email.


### Don't have your Account?
If you can't remember your password, you can recover your password on the log in page via the **Forgot your Password?** link.

If you can't remember your email address used for the account you can contact us at **sales&commat;west-wind.com** and provide us with one or more of the following from your original order:

* Order Number
* Your Name and/or Company
* Your Email Address  
* Any other uniquely identifying information
  <small>*Note: We'll only confirm emails back to the original email or same company address.*</small>
